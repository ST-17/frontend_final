The "Mountain Kings"

Mountain Kings is a club for organizing group trips and hikes in the mountains and nature. The main goals of the club are to attract university students to outdoor activities and to give them unforgettable memories of their student years. Our every trip includes musical accompaniment, entertaining program, professional photo set, interesting acquaintances, games, contests and so much more. Despite our very short existence (six months), we have managed to achieve a lot
